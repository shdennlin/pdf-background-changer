PDF Background Changer

This tool allows you to upload a PDF and change its background color directly in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server; all processing happens locally for your privacy.
Upload a PDF, pick a background color, and download your updated PDF. All processing happens in your browser.
Powered by pdf-lib. Only the background color is changed; all other content remains untouched.
